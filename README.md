## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

- **Installer doesn’t start**: Check if your antivirus is blocking it. Temporarily disable it and try again.
- **App won’t open**: Restart your computer and launch site-builder again.
- **Login problems**: Ensure your internet is working. Reset your password if you can’t sign in.
- **Website not updating**: Refresh the preview window or restart the app.
- **Payment not processing**: Check your internet connection and your payment method details.
